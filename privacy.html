Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Overview

PaceAI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

Health & Fitness Data (On-Device Only)

  • Location data — GPS coordinates during active workouts to calculate your route, pace, and distance. This data remains on your device.
  • Heart rate — Streamed from Apple Watch via HealthKit for real-time heart rate zone coaching.
  • Motion data — Cadence and step data from device sensors during active workouts.
  • HealthKit data — With your explicit permission: resting heart rate, VO2 Max, weight, and height to personalize your coaching experience.

Profile Information

  • Name, age, weight, height, and running experience level — stored locally on your device.
  • Coaching preferences and workout history — stored locally using Apple's encrypted SwiftData.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect location data for advertising or tracking.
  • We do not sell, share, or rent personal data to third parties.
  • We do not use health data for advertising purposes.
  • We do not create user profiles for marketing.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA).

HealthKit

PaceAI uses Apple HealthKit to read fitness data and write workout records. Per Apple's requirements:

  • HealthKit data is never used for advertising or sold to data brokers.
  • HealthKit data is not shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
  • HealthKit data is used solely for the app's health and fitness coaching purpose.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's encrypted storage mechanisms. No account creation is required. The app functions fully without an internet connection.

Optional Cloud Features

If you opt into AI-powered coaching features requiring internet connectivity, anonymized workout metrics (pace, heart rate zone, cadence) may be transmitted to generate coaching responses. No personally identifiable information is included.

Your Rights

  • Delete data: Uninstall the app to remove all local data, or use the Reset option in Settings.
  • Revoke permissions: Disable HealthKit, Location, or Microphone access in iOS Settings at any time.

Children

PaceAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated through the app.
