Features

Everything a world-class
coach does. In your ear.

PaceAI combines GPS tracking, AI intelligence, and natural voice to give you an experience no running app has ever delivered.

🎙️

Real-Time Voice Coaching

Natural voice cues that react to your pace, heart rate, and cadence. Not robotic announcements — real coaching that adapts every second.

🧠

AI Workout Planning

Tell PaceAI your goal and it generates a personalized plan — warmup, tempo, intervals, cooldown — tailored to your fitness level.

⌚

Apple Watch Integration

Stream heart rate from your wrist for precise zone coaching. See pace, distance, and coaching cues on your Apple Watch.

💬

Voice Conversation

Ask your coach mid-run: "How am I doing?" or say "I'm tired" and the workout adapts instantly. Natural two-way conversation.

📴

Works Offline

On-device coaching engine runs without internet. Take PaceAI to trails, treadmills, or anywhere with no cell signal.

📊

Smart Analytics

Post-run splits, HR zone charts, pace trends, and AI-generated summaries with actionable training recommendations.
